Girl-talk-Girl


Thanks for using the Girl-talk-Girl mobile application! We ask that you join us in sharing your experiences, thoughts, and resources on the subject of violence against girls and women by creating and sharing a story with the world, and by visiting us on our website www.girltalkgirl.org for more information. Please follow us on @girl_talk_girl on Twitter for updates too!


The content provided in this resource section was decided by the Girl-talk-Girl participants in the first workshops held in New York City, USA, and in St. Petersburg, Russia. Please email us at girl.talk.girl.project@gmail.com with links and resources you would like us to add here and/or to our website!


Girl-talk-Girl is run by Footage: Empowering youth through voice (see www.footageyouth.org) and was made through a US-Russia Peer-to-Peer Dialogue Program Award from the US State Department.
