Safe Routes and Dangerous Routes in Our City 


Do you know of the safest routes from A to B in your city? Or, for example, from a popular cafe to a metro station? Or, do you know a route that should be avoided? 


If so make it into a story and use your GPS and create an itinerary (and pop that map into your story!). Send us your story via email girl.talk.girl.project@gmail.com so we can upload the route to our website and other girls and women can download it to their Girl-talk-Girl application. Please indicate the city and country. You can add your name (or AKA) if you feel like it! 


Thanks for sharing!
